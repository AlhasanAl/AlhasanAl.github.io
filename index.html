01 / ABOUT

Where I come from, and where I'm headed

I'm an IT professional with nearly two years of hands-on support experience across Windows, Linux, and macOS: POS systems, workstations, LAN/WAN connectivity, and the people who depend on all of it. I hold a B.S. in Information Technology from Arizona State University.

I currently work in Patient Access at Endeavor Health, managing patient data across multiple EHR systems under strict HIPAA requirements. It's taught me something most IT candidates never learn: how clinical staff actually use the systems we support, and what it costs them when those systems fail.

I'm returning to IT full time: help desk, systems, or network support. I keep a home lab running Kali Linux, AWS environments, and Cisco network simulations, because the fastest way to understand a system is to build it and then try to break it.
